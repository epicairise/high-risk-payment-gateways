FAQs

Q: What makes a business “high-risk”?
A: Industries with high chargeback rates, regulatory scrutiny, or refund risk are typically labeled high-risk by processors.

Q: Can I use Stripe or PayPal?
A: Most high-risk businesses are either banned or at risk of sudden shutdown on platforms like Stripe or PayPal. Specialized providers are safer.

Q: How do I choose the right gateway?
A: Pick based on your vertical, average ticket size, country, and whether you offer recurring billing or free trials.
